My Aim

I offer an accepting, non-judgmental and confidential service with a down-to-earth and friendly manner. I hope working with me will enable you to develop insight and understanding into your thoughts, feelings and behaviours and help you overcome your difficulties.

My Experience

I have over twenty-five years experience of working in mental health and over the last seventeen years as a Counsellor and Cognitive Behavioural Therapist (CBT). These models of therapy are recommended for a range of psychological problems by the National Institute for Care Excellence (NHS NICE guidelines). I am also a qualified supervisor.
